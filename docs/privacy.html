Privacy

What is stored

Emotion Wheel stores the emotion records you create, optional reflection fields, optional notes, capture settings and your selected colour theme. This information is stored in your browser's local storage on the current device.

What is not collected

The app does not use accounts, analytics, tracking cookies, remote databases or a server. It does not automatically send your information to anyone.

When data leaves your browser

Data leaves your browser only when you choose to export a CSV file, save a JSON backup, share a chart or table, or copy information to the clipboard. Check the destination and contents before sharing, particularly when a note includes sensitive information.

Keeping control of your data

Use Maintenance to save a JSON backup, load a previous backup or clear the current log. Clearing the log removes records from the current browser. Clearing browser site data also removes records and preferences, so save a backup first if you want to keep them.

Important limitation

This is a personal reflection tool, not a medical service, emergency service or secure clinical record. Avoid storing information you would not be comfortable keeping in local browser storage on the device you are using.
