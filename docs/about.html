About Emotion Wheel

Emotion Wheel is an accessible, single-page tool for recording and reflecting on emotions in your own browser. It is designed to make everyday entry simple while providing richer review tools for spotting patterns.

Recording

Record an emotion from the inner, middle or outer circle. Phase 1 supports simple habit-building, while Phase 2 and Custom modes add optional reflection information and notes.

Reviewing

Logs, tallies, written insights, accessible charts and matching tables help you review information by day, week, month or all time.

Managing your information

Your entries remain in the current browser unless you deliberately export, copy or share them. Maintenance provides CSV exports and JSON backup, merge and replacement options.
